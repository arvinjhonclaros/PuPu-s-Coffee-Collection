Cappuccino

The Cappuccino we now know as develop in italy from Vienese drink "Kapuziner" brewed coffee, mixed with milk and cream.

Americano

Americano contains espresso and hot water. Espresso being the operative word, which uses a quick extraction method.

Caffe Latte

A Caffè Latte is a milk coffee that is a made up of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and thin layer of frothed milk on top.

Caffe Mocha

A Mocha is that a shot of espresso is combined with a chocolate powder or syrup followed by milk or cream.

Macchiato

Macchiato is an espresso coffee drink with a small amount of foamed or steamed milk on top to let the flavor of the espresso.

Espresso

Espresso (ess-PRESS-oh) is a full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in “shots.”

Flat White

A Flat White is a blend of micro-foamed milk poured over a single or double shot of espresso.

Plain Coffee

Coffee that is brewed without the addition such as sugar, milk, cream, or added flavors.

Oat Latte

Lightly sweetened Oat Milk is blended and topped with cold brew for delicious iced refreshment.

Havana

Sweet, bold, spiced, Havana begins with hand pulled Espresso & sweet condensed milk.
